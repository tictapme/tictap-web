For electrical distributors

Turn your customers' stock control into a new loyalty opportunity

TicTAP Stock enables electrical distributors to offer installers, maintenance teams and professional customers a simple way to control materials across warehouses, vans and job sites using QR/NFC — no app required.

Talk about partnership See YESSS Electrical case

The problem

Your professional customers work with distributed stock — but often without real visibility

Materials in vans, small warehouses, plant rooms, construction sites and customer premises. Without a simple system to record issues, receipts and replenishments, stockouts, duplicate purchases, emergency orders and wasted time become the norm.

  • No clear visibility of what stock is in each location
  • Over-ordering "just in case", leading to duplicated stock
  • Critical material shortages detected too late
  • Time lost on manual stock counts
  • Min/max levels managed in spreadsheets or from memory
  • Emergency orders that disrupt operations and increase cost

Why it matters for the distributor

When your customer can't control their stock, you miss opportunities too

Every stockout or unplanned replenishment is an opportunity for the customer to buy wherever has availability. Help them organise their stock and you can be present at the exact moment the replenishment need arises.

More loyalty

You help the customer solve a daily operational problem. The relationship moves from transactional to service-based.

More recurrence

Replenishments stop being reactive and become driven by actual usage and minimum levels. The distributor is part of that flow.

More differentiation

You move from selling products to offering a control and efficiency solution — something most other wholesalers cannot match.

How it works

A simple way to digitalise stock without changing how technicians work

1

Create Stores

Warehouse, van, branch, plant room — each location becomes a Store with its own QR/NFC label.

2

Scan from mobile

The technician scans the QR or taps with NFC to record an issue, receipt or adjustment. No app required.

3

Instant update

The system updates stock in real time and reports to the central portal with full movement history.

4

Automatic replenishment

When stock drops below minimum, the distributor, nominated supplier or purchasing team receives an automatic notification. Stock replenished without emergency orders.

Deployment formats

One platform, multiple formats for every working environment

Smart Van

For engineers, contractors and mobile teams. Track all van stock in real time. Automate replenishment directly from the local distributor branch. Prevent lost tools and unplanned downtime.

Smart Portal

Centralised online dashboard. Real-time visibility across all vans, stores, teams and locations. Replenishment alerts, spend tracking, usage trends and export for audits and financial planning.

Smart Store

For workshops, depots and customer stores. Smart bins, cabinets or shelving for complete stock control. Engineers book items out with QR/NFC and the system automatically monitors reorder thresholds.

Partnership models

Three ways to work with TicTAP

Model 1

Pilots with key accounts

The distributor proposes TicTAP Stock to professional accounts with warehouses, vans or recurring maintenance needs. We validate the model in a controlled environment before scaling.

Model 2

Digital value-added service

The distributor offers TicTAP as a complementary tool to improve control, replenishment and traceability across their professional customer base.

Model 3

Connected replenishment

The customer uses TicTAP to detect replenishment needs and the distributor enters that commercial flow naturally. The local branch provides installation, support, system adjustments and ongoing stock supply.

Validated case
